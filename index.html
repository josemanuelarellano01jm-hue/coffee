Origin

Born in the Mountains

We Don't Blend Away Character

"Our relationship with producers goes beyond fair trade. We visit each farm personally, cupping dozens of samples until we find the lot that stops the conversation."

Every bean is hand-picked at peak ripeness, processed within hours, and stored in climate-controlled conditions. We pay 4–10x commodity prices—not as charity, but as recognition of true quality.

The result is a portfolio of coffees as distinct as the soils they come from. Now perfectly preserved in recyclable aluminum capsules compatible with your Nespresso® system.

Process

From Cherry to Capsule

01. Source

We buy only auction lots, Cup of Excellence winners, and farm-direct micro-lots scored 87+ by certified Q-Graders.

02. Roast

Roasted in 5kg batches on vintage Probat drums. No automation—every batch is monitored by a master roaster.

03. Preserve

Immediately flushed with nitrogen and sealed in infinitely recyclable aluminum to lock in aromatics for 18 months.
